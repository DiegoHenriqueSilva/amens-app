# App Business Context - Améns

## 📌 What is Améns?

Améns is a faith-based social platform designed to connect people through prayers, spiritual support, and community interaction.

The app allows users to:
- Share prayer requests
- Support others through prayers ("Amém")
- Engage with a faith-driven community
- Express spirituality in a safe and respectful environment

---

## 🎯 Business Strategy

The product focuses on:

- Building a strong engaged community
- Encouraging daily interaction through spiritual content
- Creating emotional connection between users
- Scaling organically via social and religious networks

Future monetization strategies may include:
- Premium features (highlighted prayers, personalization)
- Partnerships with religious organizations
- Donations or sponsorships

---

## 👥 Target Audience

- Christians and spiritually engaged individuals
- People seeking emotional support
- Users interested in community-driven faith interactions
- Mobile-first users (primarily)

---

## ✝️ Religious Context

- The platform is Christian-oriented but can be inclusive
- Language and UX should always be respectful and non-offensive
- Avoid political or controversial religious debates
- Focus on positivity, empathy, and support

---

## ⚠️ Content Sensitivity

Due to the nature of user-generated content:
- Sensitive information must be handled carefully
- Moderation is a key concern
- Respect privacy and avoid exposure of personal data
